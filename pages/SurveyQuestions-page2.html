Operational Readiness Survey: Serve

1. Does the organization have a theory of change that connects its programs and services with the community issues the nonprofit is addressing?
1b. Do each of your nonprofit programs and services have a logic model developed that contains inputs, activities, outputs, and impact outcome measures?
2. Has your organization developed short, intermediate, long-term impact outcome measures related to the logic models? Are you tracking and reporting on these measures on a regular basis?
3. Does your organization have a client/case management system? If so, does that system have integration capability with community platform?
4. Do you have a solid technology foundation at your organization?
5. Do you have productivity tools that allow you to save time and be effective?
